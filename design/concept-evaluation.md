- Editorial lens: 3.90 / 5.00

## Decision

Selected direction: **Evidence grid**.

It is the only concept that gives equal visual weight to review, tags and comparison while remaining legible at a 320 x 160 preview size. The final design uses only original geometric primitives and text; it contains no third-party logos, copied interfaces or real product data.
